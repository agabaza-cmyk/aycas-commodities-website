12 · Submission & Next Steps§ Return instructions

Return the completed application with certified copies of licences, insurance schedules and bank confirmation to trade@aycascommodities.com. AYCAS will review, take references, and issue a formal appointment letter setting out scope, authorities, fee basis, liability terms and SLA to successful applicants. Vetting typically completes within seven working days.
