AYCAS Commodities — Company Profile.

AYCAS Commodities is a Zimbabwean commodities trading firm based in Harare. We run regional agricultural imports into Zimbabwe and mineral exports out of Zimbabwe to international refiners and smelters. A small Harare-based team handles the full trade lifecycle — origination, quality, contract, logistics, finance and documentary compliance — under one roof.

3 · The Service§ Full trade lifecycle

AYCAS handles the full trade lifecycle in-house on both imports and exports. One point of contact, one line of accountability.

  • 01 Origination & sourcing. Direct relationships with regional producers and with licensed Zimbabwean mines. Farm, mill and mine-site inspections done in person.
  • 02 Quality assurance & inspection. SGS, Bureau Veritas or Intertek independent inspection at loading on both sides. Retained samples held 60 days. Weighbridge verification at loading and at delivery.
  • 03 Contract negotiation. GAFTA for grains, FOSFA for oilseeds, ICUMSA-referenced terms for brown sugar, standard MMCZ-framed terms for minerals.
  • 04 Logistics & freight. SADC road and rail corridors for agricultural imports. Regional port corridors for mineral exports. Fleet vetting, vessel nomination and customs clearance handled in-house.
  • 05 Trade finance. Letters of credit (confirmed and unconfirmed), documentary collections and structured payment arrangements aligned to counterparty banking relationships.
  • 06 Documentary compliance. MMCZ permits and CD1 forms on mineral exports. Import permits, customs declarations, phytosanitary certificates and exchange-control authorisations on agricultural imports.
  • 07 Risk management. KYC and sanctions screening before contract. Marine cargo, war-and-strikes, inland transit and credit insurance placed per shipment or per relationship as warranted.

5 · Execution Cadence§ Cycle notes

Agricultural import cycle

A standard regional grain import runs on a four-day operational cycle from counterparty confirmation to cargo released at a Zimbabwean port of entry — origin loading and inspection, corridor transit, border clearance, and delivery to the buyer's nominated receiving point.

Mineral export cycle

Every mineral shipment is cleared per-cargo through the Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe. Assay and tonnage are declared before loading, the sales contract is registered, and an MMCZ export permit is issued shipment by shipment.

6 · Compliance Posture§ Regulatory framework

Mineral exports
MMCZ (Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe) — export permits issued per shipment; assay and tonnage declared; sales contracts registered.
Agricultural imports
AMA (Agricultural Marketing Authority) and GMB (Grain Marketing Board) alignment per product and period; phytosanitary certification at origin, verified at border by the Department of Plant Quarantine Services.
Customs & tax
ZIMRA — import declarations at Beitbridge, Chirundu, Plumtree or Kazungula; export declarations and certificates of origin on mineral shipments; VAT and tax clearance current.
Exchange control
RBZ (Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe) — CD1 forms on export proceeds; import forex authorisations through RBZ-authorised dealers.
Labour & statutory
NSSA-registered; statutory contributions current; operating under Zimbabwean labour law.
Counterparty screening
KYC and UBO file on every counterparty; sanctions screening against OFAC, HM Treasury, EU consolidated list and UN Security Council; PEP and adverse-media via commercial provider; periodic re-screening over the life of the relationship.
Contract basis
GAFTA for grains, FOSFA for oilseeds, ICUMSA-referenced terms for brown sugar, standard MMCZ-framed terms for minerals. Incoterms 2020. GAFTA, FOSFA or LME arbitration as applicable.

7 · Contract Discipline§ Execution controls

  • 01 Independent inspection at loading. SGS, Bureau Veritas or Intertek nominated per shipment. Certificate of Analysis signed off before documents are released.
  • 02 Weighbridge verification. Tonnage confirmed at loading and again at delivery. Not taken on trust.
  • 03 Retained samples. Sealed samples held 60 days post-delivery for dispute reference.
  • 04 Marine & inland cargo cover. Placed per shipment under Institute Cargo Clauses A where available; war, strikes and inland transit cover added where routing warrants.
