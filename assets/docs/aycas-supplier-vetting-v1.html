5 · Licences & Compliance§ Right to operate

Tick each item attached and record the evidence reference (document number, date, issuing authority). Items marked REQ are mandatory; COND items apply where relevant to the commodity.

  • Trading / dealer licence for the commodity e.g. GMB grain dealer licence, minerals dealer licence. REQ   
  • MMCZ registration Required for all mineral exports from Zimbabwe. COND   
  • GMAZ membership / grain buyer registration Maize, wheat, soya, sorghum suppliers. COND   
  • ZIMRA tax clearance (ITF 263) current REQ   
  • EMA environmental authorisation / EIA certificate REQ   
  • Health & phytosanitary certificate DVS / Plant Quarantine — applies to grains, dairy, specialty agri. COND   
  • Labour compliance — NSSA registration, minimum-wage evidence REQ   
  • Local content / indigenisation declaration where applicable COND
