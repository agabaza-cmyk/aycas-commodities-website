03 · Import into Zimbabwe

Specialty agri, to industrial spec.

Sugar, tomato paste, orange concentrate and baobab pulp — food-grade ingredients imported into Zimbabwean industrial processors, beverage manufacturers, confectioners, bakeries and nutraceutical formulators. Contracted on Certificate of Analysis with full traceability.

Overview

Processing-grade ingredients, properly documented.

Zimbabwean food and beverage processors work to international ingredient specifications that local supply cannot always deliver consistently. AYCAS sources from audited producers in Southern Africa, the EU and North Africa — contracted on Certificate of Analysis with retained samples, batch traceability and full documentary clearance.

03a

Sugar — Raw and refined.

Raw and refined sugar imported from regional producers — primarily South African mills — into Zimbabwe for industrial refiners, beverage producers, confectioners and wholesale distributors. Contracts referenced to ICUMSA grading with independent QA at loading.

Specifications
  • Raw cane sugar · ICUMSA 600–1200
  • Refined white sugar · ICUMSA 45
  • Moisture ≤ 0.04%
  • 50 kg bags · bulk on request
Sources: Southern African regional mills
Buyers: Zimbabwean industrial refiners, beverage producers, confectioners, distributors
03b

Tomato paste — Industrial grade.

Double-concentrated and triple-concentrated tomato paste imported into Zimbabwean sauce manufacturers, ready-meal producers, food-service distributors and retail repackers. Specified on Brix concentration, colour grade (Bostwick viscosity and a/b ratio) and processing method (HTST, cold-break vs hot-break).

Specifications
  • 28/30 Brix double-concentrate · most-common industrial grade
  • 31/33 Brix triple-concentrate on request
  • Colour · Bostwick / a·b ratio per buyer spec
  • Hot-break / cold-break process · per application
  • 220 kg aseptic drums · 5 kg catering tins · retail formats
Sources: Italy · Portugal · China · Egypt
Buyers: Sauce manufacturers, ready-meal producers, food-service, repackers
03c

Orange concentrate — Frozen FCOJ.

Frozen Concentrated Orange Juice (FCOJ) imported for Zimbabwean beverage bottlers, juice manufacturers and dilute-to-taste product lines. 65 Brix standard with Brazilian and South African origins; aseptic filling for shelf-stable pasteurised variants available on request.

Specifications
  • Frozen Concentrated Orange Juice · 65 Brix typical
  • Acidity (citric) per spec · pulp content per buyer
  • Single-strength pasteurised available on request
  • 200 kg drums · 1000 L flexitanks · IBC totes
  • Cold-chain controlled through delivery
Sources: Brazil · South Africa
Buyers: Beverage bottlers, juice manufacturers, dilute-to-taste formulators
03d

Baobab pulp — Food and nutraceutical grade.

Dehydrated baobab fruit pulp — high in natural vitamin C, fibre and antioxidants — for the nutraceutical, bakery premix, beverage and specialty-food sectors. Contracted to vitamin-C content, moisture spec and microbiological clearance; organic and fair-trade certification available from source where the buyer requires it.

Specifications
  • Natural dehydrated powder · no additives
  • Vitamin C content · 100–400 mg / 100 g (natural variation)
  • Moisture ≤ 10% · fibre 40–55%
  • Organic & fair-trade certified on request
  • 25 kg food-grade paper-poly bags · bulk bags on request
Sources: Southern African baobab belt
Buyers: Nutraceutical, bakery premix, beverage, specialty-food
Framework

Food-grade chain, end-to-end.

Every specialty-agri shipment arrives with a complete traceability file and full regulatory clearance. Cold-chain where product requires it.

Origin audit
Audited processors only — ISO 22000, HACCP, BRC where destination requires it. Plant code, production date and batch number on every lot.
Testing
Certificate of Analysis per lot. Retained samples held 60 days. Microbiological panel appropriate to the product class.
Cold chain
FCOJ and temperature-sensitive product on cold-chain lanes with container-level logging. Tomato paste & sugar at ambient with humidity control.
Import clearance
Ministry of Health food-safety clearance as required. ZIMRA import declaration, duty & VAT paid. Ministry of Industry & Commerce import permits where applicable.
Contract
Incoterms 2020 — CIF / CFR Durban typically, with onward road transit into Zimbabwe. LC or documentary collection on presentation.
