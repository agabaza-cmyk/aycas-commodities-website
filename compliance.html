Compliance

Regulated, licensed, documented.

AYCAS operates under the full Zimbabwean trading, import and export regulatory framework — and screens every counterparty against the standards international buyers and regional suppliers expect.

Corporate framework

Zimbabwean incorporated.

Company registration
Registered private limited company under the Zimbabwe Companies and Other Business Entities Act [Chapter 24:31]. Entity number 56514A0282025. Incorporated 27 August 2025. Registered office at Rosedale 208–209 Sam Nujoma Street, Harare.
Tax compliance
Registered with ZIMRA (Zimbabwe Revenue Authority). VAT registration and tax clearance certificate available on request.
Exchange control
All forex flows managed under Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ) procedures — CD1 forms on export proceeds; import forex authorisations and bank-remittance compliance on imports.
Labour compliance
Operating under Zimbabwean labour law, NSSA-registered, statutory contributions current.
Mineral exports

MMCZ-licensed, per shipment.

MMCZ licensing
All mineral shipments cleared through the Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe. Export permits obtained per shipment; assay and tonnage declared to MMCZ before loading; sales contracts registered.
Customs & certificates of origin
Export declarations lodged with ZIMRA customs. Certificates of origin issued by ZIMRA or recognised chambers (ZNCC, CZI) per destination market.
Sanctions-compliant routing
End-buyer KYC and sanctions screening cleared before loading. Routing chosen to avoid sanctioned ports and flags.
Agricultural imports

ZIMRA-cleared, phytosanitary-certified.

ZIMRA customs
All import shipments declared at port of entry (Beitbridge, Chirundu, Plumtree or Kazungula), duty and VAT paid in line with applicable Zimbabwean tariff schedules.
Phytosanitary
Phytosanitary certificates issued at origin — DAFF (South Africa), ZARI (Zambia), or the Department of Agricultural Research Services (Malawi) — and verified at the Zimbabwean border by the Department of Plant Quarantine Services.
Import permits & forex
Import permits obtained from the Ministry of Industry and Commerce where required. Forex authorisations arranged through RBZ-authorised dealers in line with prevailing exchange-control directives.
GMB / AMA alignment
Grain imports coordinated with the Grain Marketing Board and Agricultural Marketing Authority per product and period.
Internal governance

Disciplined operating model.

AYCAS operates under a formal separation-of-duties governance framework — order origination, approval, supplier vetting, operations and finance each sit with distinct functions, with executive sign-off where the standard demands it.

The full operating procedures and control matrix are documented internally and available to counterparties, banks and institutional reviewers on request, under NDA.

Counterparty screening

KYC, sanctions, ongoing monitoring.

AYCAS performs counterparty clearance on both sides of every trade before contracting. Monitoring continues across the life of the relationship.

KYC file
Incorporation documents · UBO declaration · signatory authorisation · bank references.
Sanctions screening
OFAC (US) · HM Treasury (UK) · EU consolidated list · UN Security Council. Cleared before loading.
PEP & adverse-media
Screened via commercial provider. Escalation to board-level review on any amber flag.
Ongoing monitoring
Periodic re-screening across the life of the trading relationship. Contract terminable on sanction designation or material adverse finding.
Contractual & insurance

Familiar paper, covered cargo.

Grains & oilseeds
GAFTA (Grain and Feed Trade Association) contracts for grains; FOSFA contracts for oils and oilseed-derived products.
Sugar
ICUMSA-referenced terms for brown-sugar trade.
Minerals
Standard MMCZ-framed terms. Independent inspection (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek). CIF, FOB, CFR — Incoterms 2020.
Dispute resolution
GAFTA, FOSFA or LME arbitration as applicable; substantive law as agreed in contract.
Insurance
Marine cargo insurance placed per shipment (Institute Cargo Clauses A where available). Credit insurance for open-account trades where warranted. War, strikes, riots and civil commotion cover where routing warrants. Inland transit cover on regional road and rail movements.

Documents on request.

Counterparties and KYC reviewers — the full AYCAS KYC pack, tax clearance, bank references and insurance certificates are available to serious enquirers on request.
