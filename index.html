Commodities · two directions of trade

What we trade.

AYCAS runs two commodity lines flowing in opposite directions — agricultural imports into Zimbabwe from regional origins, and mineral exports from Zimbabwe to international counterparties. The discipline does not change with the cargo.

01 · Source & Import

Grains & Pulses

Maize (GMO and Non-GMO, white and yellow), sugar beans, soya beans and peanuts — sourced from Zimbabwean commercial growers (particularly Non-GMO white maize) and regional origins for blue-chip Zimbabwean industrial buyers.

ZW · SA · ZM · MW → Zimbabwe
02 · Import

Dairy Powders

Full Cream Milk Powder (FCMP), Skim Milk Powder (SMP), Anhydrous Milk Fat (AMF) and whey powder for blue-chip Zimbabwean industrial buyers. Food-grade, audited origin.

SA · EU · NZ → Zimbabwe
03 · Import

Specialty Agri

Brown sugar, tomato paste, orange concentrate and baobab pulp — specialty ingredients for blue-chip Zimbabwean industrial buyers.

Regional · EU · Americas → Zimbabwe
04 · Export

Tungsten

Scheelite and wolframite concentrates from Zimbabwean deposits, shipped under MMCZ licensing to tier-one international refiners.

WO₃ ≥ 40% · MMCZ-permitted
05 · Export

Chrome

Chrome ore and concentrates from licensed Great Dyke mines, shipped via regional ports to tier-one international stainless-steel and ferrochrome counterparties.

Cr₂O₃ 38–46% · Lumpy / friable / concentrate
06 · Either direction

On Enquiry

Additional commodities — tobacco, lithium, manganese, soybean meal, rice, fertilisers — sourced or placed against firm enquiry in either direction.

Case-by-case · executable or declined
How we work

Full lifecycle, under one accountability line.

AYCAS Commodities handles the full trade lifecycle in-house — origination, quality assurance, contract, logistics, trade finance and documentary compliance — under a disciplined internal governance model.

The operational detail is reserved for counterparties in the room. Serious enquirers receive our corporate profile and, where warranted, controlled visibility of our operations manual under NDA.

About

A trading firm built on quiet competence.

AYCAS Commodities was incorporated in Harare on 27 August 2025 to bring disciplined commodities trading to the flows that matter regionally — agricultural imports into Zimbabwe and mineral exports from Zimbabwe.

Our principals bring combined experience across engineering, operations and Zimbabwean commercial practice. We contract, we inspect, we ship, we settle — nothing gets subcontracted, and every counterparty deals with one accountable team.

Compliance

Regulated, licensed, documented.

AYCAS operates under the full Zimbabwean regulatory framework on both imports and exports — and screens every counterparty before contracting.

01

Zimbabwean incorporated

Registered Pvt Ltd under the Companies and Other Business Entities Act. Entity 56514A0282025. Incorporated 27 August 2025.

02

Import & export regimes

MMCZ permits per mineral shipment. ZIMRA customs, phytosanitary and regulatory alignment on agricultural imports per product and period.

03

Contract discipline

GAFTA and FOSFA for agricultural lines, MMCZ-framed terms for minerals, Incoterms 2020. Independent inspection and CoA per lot.

04

KYC & sanctions screening

Every counterparty cleared through KYC, AML and OFAC / EU / UK / UN sanctions screening before contract is signed.

05

Internal governance

Disciplined separation-of-duties operating model. Detailed procedures available to counterparties under NDA.
